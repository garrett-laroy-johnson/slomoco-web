SIGN STEALING | Megan Young

an exploration of human-drone cohabitation

Sign Stealing uses creative practice to consider the complex issues of human-drone cohabitation. This approach synergizes kinesthetic exploration, scientific research, and political activism through public forums. Engagements utilize contemplative education strategies and participant generated protocols to express existing needs, desires, or assumptions around the topic. Early stage research will be shared as: poethical survey, relationship profiles, cartographic competition, and rudimentary guidance systems for hide and seek operations.
